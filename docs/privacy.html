Privacy Policy — SuperCalcPro

Last updated: March 29, 2026

SuperCalcPro ("we", "our", or "the app") is a scientific calculator app for iOS. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

SuperCalcPro itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All calculation history is stored exclusively on your device and is never uploaded to any server.

The app contains a banner advertisement powered by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect the following information to serve personalised or non-personalised ads:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA) — only if you grant permission via the App Tracking Transparency prompt
  • Device type, operating system version, and general location (country/region)
  • App interaction events (ad impressions and clicks)

2. App Tracking Transparency (iOS 14.5+)

When you first open SuperCalcPro, iOS will ask whether you allow the app to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites. If you allow tracking, AdMob will use your IDFA to show more relevant personalised ads. If you deny tracking, non-personalised ads will be shown instead. You can change this choice at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

3. Third-Party Services

SuperCalcPro uses Google AdMob for advertising. Google's privacy policy is available at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Storage

All data generated by the app (calculation history, settings) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. It is never transmitted to external servers.

5. Children's Privacy

SuperCalcPro is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not contain any content directed specifically at children.

6. Your Rights

You may delete all locally stored data at any time by uninstalling the app. To opt out of personalised advertising, deny tracking permission in the iOS settings or in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top.
